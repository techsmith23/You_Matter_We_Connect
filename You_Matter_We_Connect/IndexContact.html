About Us

Our goal is for students to become immersed in the college experience. Whether it's frat parties, nail-biting hackathons, or jawdropping performances, we want everyone to know that there's something going on every second. If you want to get involved, we're here for you. If you want free food, we're here for you. If you want to relax after a stressful week, we're here for you. We hope you can fully appreciate the performance of our app.
